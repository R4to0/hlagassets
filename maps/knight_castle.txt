INSTALLATION:

1. 	Run Winzip and unzip knight_castle.zip.

2. 	Open the knight_castle folder and place the knight_castle.bsp, 				knight_castle.res, and knight_castle.txt in your valve/maps folder.  

3.	Place the 6 (six) odin_.tga files into your valve/gfx/env folder.

4.	Place the wav file in a folder called castle1 and place that in your valve/sound 		folder.

5.	Place the blue1.spr in your valve/sprites folder.
