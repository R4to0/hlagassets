INSTALLATION:

1. 	Run Winzip and unzip elite.zip.

2. 	Open the folder and place the elite.bsp, elite.txt and elite.res in valve/maps. 		  
3.	Place the 6 (six) planet.tga files into your valve/gfx/env folder.

4.	Place the wav file in a folder called elite and place that in your valve/sound 			folder.
