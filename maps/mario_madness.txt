INSTALLATION:

1. 	Run Winzip and unzip mario_madness.zip.

2. 	Open the mario_kart folder and place the mario_madness.bsp, 				mario_madness.res, and mario_madness.tx in your valve/maps folder.  

3.	Place the 6 (six) grass.tga files into your valve/gfx/env folder.

4.	Put the 2 wavs in a folder called mario4 and put it in the valve/sound folder.

5.	Place the cloud.spr and spinning_coin.spr in your valve/sprites folder.

6.	Place cannon_guy.mdl inside the valve/models folder.
